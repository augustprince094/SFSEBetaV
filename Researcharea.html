OUR RESEARCH THEMES



The core of our research group is to conduct or develop tools for sustainability assessment at the CONSUMER and INDUSTRIAL level.

At the CONSUMER level we are focused on bridging the environmental sustainability and nutrition.

At the INDUSTRAIL level we are focused employing life cycle sustainability assessment tools to evaluate the performance of agri-food and bio-product systems.

In achieving this, we leverage digital innovations, machine learning, computational modelling, and human health impact modeling to drive a sustainable and resilient food system.
